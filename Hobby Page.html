What Does Michael Do For Fun?

His Hobbies

Interview: 1. What are your favorite colors?
Red and blue but mainly just red.
2. What are your favorite animals?
The orca whale is my favorite animal.
3. What do you do to relax?
Usually just listen to music.
4. What are your favorite things to do?
I like to go on walks, go to the movie theater, and travel.
5. What is your dream job?
Either to own my own music business for people to take music lessons there or to teach piano at a university.
6. Are you a religious person at all?
I’m Christian, and my faith is very important to me.
7. What are your favorite foods?
Any Mexican or Japanese food is fine by me.
8. How old are you?
24.
9. What are your favorite books?
The Bible and The Secret Life of Bees are my favorite books.
10. What are your biggest strengths and biggest weaknesses?
I’m good at being friendly and inclusive with friends and strangers and anyone. My biggest weaknesses are being disorganized and lacking focus.
11. What are your greatest accomplishments?
My greatest accomplishment so far is surviving grad school, which I’m about to graduate from, with a degree in Piano Performance.
